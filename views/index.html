DAWN

     DAWN is an acronym which stands for the Depression Anxiety and Wellness Network. Originally this project was developed by a team of four students a few hours each day during our computer science and software engineering courses. The intent and original mission plan were simple in the beginning; develop a resource that students of Point Pleasant Borough High School would be able to use whenever they were feeling negatively effected.
     Since then, our team now consists of three members: two co-developers and one project manager. However, since all team members are currently undergraduate students in college, our main priority is providing the most effective experience for our users. While growing a larger platform may have been put on the backburner for now, there is no doubt that we will be trying to expand and offer this software to all people who could benefit from it.
